Problem Statement

The Problem Facing 12 Lakh+ Indian Retail Pharmacies

❌ High Expiry Stock Losses

Chemists lose 5-10% of annual inventory value due to unmonitored drug expiry and lack of FEFO batch tracking.

❌ Internet Outage Downtime

Cloud-only POS systems crash during network drops in Tier 2/3 cities, causing direct sales loss.

❌ Slow Prescription Typing

Staff wastes hours manually typing handwritten doctor prescription names and dosages.

❌ Legacy Software Cost

Traditional desktop software charge heavy upfront costs (₹15k-₹25k) + expensive hidden module fees.

Solution

The Solution — EasyPharma Platform

⚡ Offline-Cloud Hybrid Sync

100% zero-downtime offline billing. Auto-syncs data to cloud when internet is available.

📸 AI Prescription Scanner

Instantly extracts handwritten/printed doctor prescriptions and matches stock in seconds.

⏳ FEFO Expiry Loss Control

Proactive alerts for near-expiry batches, reducing dead stock wastage by up to 80%.

📲 WhatsApp Invoicing

Sends instant e-bills and automated refill reminders directly to patients on WhatsApp.
